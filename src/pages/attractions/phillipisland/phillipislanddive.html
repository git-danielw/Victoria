Sea Scooters

Take a tour like no other on Phillip Island. First you will take a tour on our 7.5m Dive boat to arrive and one of Phillip Islands Iconic dive sites from there you will enjoy a fully guided snorkel tour with a PADI qualified professional who will help develop and master basic snorkel skills and instruct you on how to operate a Yamaha Sea Scooter. The Yamaha Sea Scooters are a fun way to explore a dive site allowing you to cruise along on the surface as well as dive down and get up close and personal with all the action below.
